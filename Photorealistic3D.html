The Problem

Visualizing the causes of flooding in Cité Soleil

Cité Soleil, a vibrant and bustling community, has endured countless challenges, but none more devastating than the floods that disrupt lives year after year. When the rains come, the streets transform into rivers, sweeping away homes, belongings, and hope. cause lies miles away, upstream, where deforestation has left the land barren. Without trees to hold the soil, water rushes down unchecked, overwhelming Cité Soleil. Compounding this, the plastic waste littering the streets blocks drainage systems, turning a natural event into a human-made disaster.
